# Advertising Thinkers Taxonomy

## Creatives, Strategists, and Hybrids

This document categorizes influential advertising and strategy thinkers into three distinct operating modes:

1. Pure Out-and-Out Creatives
2. Pure Out-and-Out Strategists
3. Creative–Strategy Hybrids

Each entry includes style, tagline, color palette, and a Quote / Mantra.

- Quoted text indicates a **canonical or high-confidence quote**
- Plain text indicates a **stroke mantra** (synthesized operating principle)

---

## Generator Design Note

A powerful invocation pattern is to select:
- one Pure Creative
- one Pure Strategist
- one Hybrid

and force the system to resolve the tension between them.

This is where originality emerges.
